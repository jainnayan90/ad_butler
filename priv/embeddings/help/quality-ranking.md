# Meta's quality ranking

Meta assigns three rankings to ads with enough data: quality_ranking,
engagement_rate_ranking, and conversion_rate_ranking. Each ranking compares
your ad to others competing for the same audience.

Tiers (most → least desirable):
- `above_average`
- `average`
- `below_average_10_percent`
- `below_average_20_percent`
- `below_average_35_percent`
- `unknown` (Meta has not yet computed)

Falling from `above_average` to anything lower within 7 days is a fatigue
signal. Falling to `below_average_35_percent` means Meta is actively
deprioritizing your ad in auctions — change creative or pause.
